Hello Kitty by Sanrio

Hello Kitty has been the face of the company 'Sanrio' since its creation in 1974. She was born in the suburbs of London; she lives with her parents and twin sister. Hello kitty is a friendly and compassionate young girl who enjoys baking, playing piano, and spending time with her friends and family, including her sister, Mimmy.

History

Since her creation Hello Kitty has undergone significant evolution, reflecting changes in design, branding, and cultural relevance. Initially introduced as a simple character for children, her minimalist design—with a round face, big eyes, and a signature bow—quickly captivated audiences. Over the years, Hello Kitty expanded her reach beyond toys and stationery to become a versatile global brand, appearing in fashion, beauty products, and collaborations with major companies. Her character has also been adapted to resonate with different age groups, embodying themes of empowerment and creativity. As a cultural icon, Hello Kitty has embraced various trends while maintaining her core values of friendship and kindness, ensuring her continued popularity across generations.
